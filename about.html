Learn more about me…

In short, I am a self-starting artist/designer who really wants to do work that matters. I was formally educated at the University of North Dakota, where I received my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design & New Art Media, equipping me with the tools that I now use every day.

Take a look at this

Although the first title I would claim is graphic designer, I love to be involved in many other aspects of both the creative and technical worlds. As an analytical thinker and problem solver, I'm all about web development, especially front-end coding and user experience design. The desire to understand how and why things work drives me to learn new things and stretch my boundaries —its just ingrained in my D.N.A.

I will always be drawn to the new frontiers in marketing, the cohesiveness of brand development, and the nuance of typography and visual communication. I thoroughly enjoy photography and copy writing, in addition to the processes of brainstorming, collaboration, and strategic planning,

My wife Jessica, our little daughter Eloise, and I live in Grand Forks, ND. Jessie keeps a wonderful blog about our family over at Blogspot. Feel free to check it out if you want to learn more about us.

My Process

Utilizing a creative process is a great idea. It maximizes efficiency and provides a great framework in which to create precise solutions for a multitude of visual challenges. In a world where anyone who opens Photoshop can consider themselves a designer, there is a lot of poor design. Here's the design process I use.

  • Define & Research

  • Strategize & Brainstorm

  • Construct & Polish

  • Deploy & Assess
